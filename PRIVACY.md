# Folio — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-14_

Folio ("we", "us") helps you plan, grow, and track your personal finances. This
policy explains what we store and how you stay in control.

## What we store

- **Your account**: email address and authentication credentials, handled by our
  authentication provider (Supabase).
- **Your plan data**: the figures you enter — income, budget split, investments,
  net-worth items, goals, subscriptions, and transaction log. This is stored so
  your plan syncs across your devices.

We do **not** connect to your bank, and we do not sell your data.

## Where it's stored

- A copy is kept **on your device** (local storage) so the app works offline.
- A copy is synced to our cloud database so you can log in from any device. Each
  user can only access their own data, enforced by row-level security.

## Your controls

- **Export**: download a full JSON backup of your data at any time
  (More → Data → Export).
- **Import**: restore from a backup file.
- **Delete account**: permanently deletes your account and all associated cloud
  data (More → Account → Delete account). This cannot be undone.

## Third parties

- **Supabase** — authentication and database hosting.
- **Google** — optional sign-in, if you choose to use it.
